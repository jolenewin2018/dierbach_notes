 Figure 2-19: Operator Precedence of Arithemetic Operators in Python
*********************************************************************

+-----------------------------+--------------------------+
|  Operator                   |           Associativity  |
+=============================+==========================+
|  ``** (exponentiation)``    |           right-to-left  |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------+
|  ``- (negation)``           |           left-to-right  |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------+
|  ``*, /, //, %``            |           left-to-right  |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------+
|  ``+, -``                   |           left-to-right  |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------+
